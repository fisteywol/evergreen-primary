About Evergreen Primary School

Discover our vision, values, and commitment to excellence

Our Vision

Evergreen Primary School is dedicated to providing a nurturing, inclusive, and stimulating environment where every child can thrive. We believe in fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our Values

Respect, Responsibility, Resilience, and Reflection form the cornerstone of our school community. We encourage our pupils to be kind, curious, and confident learners who contribute positively to society.

Our Curriculum

We offer a broad and balanced curriculum that challenges and inspires. From core subjects to creative arts and physical education, we ensure every child has opportunities to discover their talents and develop new skills.

Our Community

We work closely with parents, carers, and the local community to create a supportive network around our pupils. Together, we celebrate achievements, support challenges, and build lasting relationships.
